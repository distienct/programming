Make a chocolate chip cookie in 3 steps

  1. Step 1
  2. Step 2
  3. Step 3

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla
  • 2 large eggs
  • a whackload of butter
  • 2 cups of semisweet milk chocochips
  • 2 cups flour

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In small bowl, whisk together everything.

Step 2

Drop heaping tablespoon-size balls of dough about 2 inches apart on aking sheets lined with parchment.

Step 3

Bake til golden!
